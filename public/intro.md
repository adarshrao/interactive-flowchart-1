Exploring Alternatives is an interactive journey through ideas, values, and practices that challenge dominant models of development. It begins from a simple recognition that many of the systems shaping our lives today are creating ecological breakdown, deep inequality, and social disconnection, and asks what other ways of living might be possible.

The flowchart brings together ways of thinking and doing that are rooted in justice, care for the Earth, and collective well-being. It introduces the Flower of Transformation as a framework to understand how ecological integrity, social justice, democracy, economic diversity, and cultural plurality are deeply interconnected.

Moving beyond theory, Exploring Alternatives also highlights real-world case studies from different contexts, where communities are reclaiming autonomy, protecting the commons, and shaping their own futures. These are not perfect solutions or finished models, but living processes grounded in place, shaped by history, and driven by shared values.

# How to Use

Explore the chart yourself by selecting any visible items and moving along step by step.
